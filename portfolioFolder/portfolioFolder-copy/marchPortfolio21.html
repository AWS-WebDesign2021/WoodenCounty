March: Guide to the Pacific Northwest

This project serves true to its title; it was designed to be a resource for locals and travellers alike on the happenings and festivities in the Pacific Northwest.

The homepage features an up-to-date calender of events. Ranging from the arts and history to sports and culture, the website caters to varrying areas of interest.

New features include:

  • image backgrounds
  • uniform title and navigation bar
  • hover functionality
