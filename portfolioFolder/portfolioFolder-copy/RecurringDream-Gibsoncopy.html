Dog Bites - N.Gibson

4 February, 2021

In this recollection of the dream, I'm running around the pavillion of my old Catholic school. It's daytime, it's empty, and there are two maybe three big, muscular black Dogs chasing me. Their fur is so black it's shiny. When I reach the front doors, they're locked and the Dogs catch up to me and bite only my hands. I can feel my hands experiencing sharp tingles as I stand facing the locked doors. Oh well, I suppose I could turn around and face the Dogs. We'll become friends.

Thank you.
