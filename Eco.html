1. Why be a Neighborhood Ecologist

Whether you live in one of Seattle's highest skyrises, one of Wenatchee's densest forests, or a cozy neighborhood in between, the state of the natural environment affects you. From the seemingly common Crow ahead or the types of trees planted in your yard, all of the organisms are working together through the game of life and that transfer of Energy. Living in a world with basic ecological knowledge will benefit you.
